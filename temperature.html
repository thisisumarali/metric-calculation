Conversion Techniques:

Fahrenheit to Celsius:

Formula: Celsius = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9

Context: Developed by Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit, the Fahrenheit scale is commonly used in the U.S. This conversion helps bridge the gap between Fahrenheit and Celsius for temperature measurements.

Celsius to Fahrenheit:

Formula: Fahrenheit = (Celsius * 9/5) + 32

Context: Celsius, part of the metric system, is widely used globally. This conversion facilitates the understanding of temperature readings in both Celsius and Fahrenheit.
